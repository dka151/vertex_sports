📖 Rules

  • 4 Partner Group Format - All Players play each other in 2 Sets - up to 21 Points - No Setting
  • 5 or 6 Partner Group Format - All Players play each other in 2 Sets - up to 21 Points - No Setting

  • Where only One Section in Division - Top is Winner, Second is Runner-Up

  • Final - A Standard 3 Set Match - Setting is Permitted in Final
